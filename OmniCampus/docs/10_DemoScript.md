# OmniCampus Demo Script (5 Minutes)

**Target Audience:** Investors / Product Judges.

## Intro (0:00 - 0:30)
*Presenter*: "Welcome to OmniCampus. True digital transformation for education isn't just a basic LMS—it's unifying the entire operational layer. Let me show you how we solve the daily faculty nightmare: Attendance and Engagement."

## 1. The Faculty Experience (0:30 - 1:45)
*(Screen split: Mobile App mirrored on left, Web Dashboard on right)*
*Presenter*: "I'm Professor Smith. I walk into class, open the OmniCampus mobile app, and hit 'Start Session'."
*Action*: App goes into Attendance Mode. A rotating, visually striking QR code appears on screen. 
**[WOW MOMENT #1: Anti-Fraud QR]**
*Presenter*: "Students scan this. Notice it changes every 5 seconds. If a student tries taking a picture to send it to a friend in the dorms, the code expires. What if the Wi-Fi fails? Our app immediately falls back to offline mode. Once I have signal, it syncs seamlessly to the cloud. Over 300 students marked in 20 seconds, securely."

## 2. Classroom & AI Copilot (1:45 - 2:45)
*Presenter*: "Now, class is over. On the web dashboard, I post the class materials."
*Action*: Navigates to Class Stream, uploads a PDF. Focus shifts to the "Campus Copilot" button.
*Presenter*: "I click 'Generate Quiz'. Instantly, the AI reads the PDF and drafts 5 comprehension questions. I review, click Approve, and the assignment flies out. No black-box AI; Faculty is always strictly in control."
**[WOW MOMENT #2: AI Quiz Draft]**

## 3. Communication & Compliance (2:45 - 3:45)
*Presenter*: "Switching hats. I'm the Principal." 
*Action*: Logs in as Principal. Dashboard changes entirely.
*Presenter*: "We need to announce an emergency policy change to all Engineering students. We draft it right here, toggle 'Require Acknowledgment', and fire."
*Action*: Broadcast fires. Dashboard instantly fills a real-time bar chart showing "Read" vs "Acknowledged" vs "Ignored".
*Presenter*: "Total accountability. We know exactly who hasn't seen it."

## 4. Governance Leadership Dashboard (3:45 - 5:00)
*Presenter*: "But here's the real power for leadership: The 'At-Risk' index."
*Action*: Scrolls down to the Campus Heatmap.
*Presenter*: "OmniCampus combines attendance data, assignment submission rates, and LMS engagement into an early-warning system. We see student 'John Doe' flagged in red. Why? (Clicks 'Explain'). The AI clearly states: 'John missed 3 consecutive attendance periods across multiple subjects and hasn't submitted 2 recent assignments. High risk of dropout.'"
**[WOW MOMENT #3: Explainable Early-Warning AI Dashboard]**

*Presenter*: "OmniCampus: Attendance, Classroom, Communication, Governance. All in sync. Thank you."
